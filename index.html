Program Overview

Korean Government-sponsored Volunteer Teachers Program, launched by the Ministry of Education(MOE) of Republic of Korea in 2013, dispatches certified Korean teachers to the countries with the high demand in the basic literacy education, e.g. math, science, ICT, and Korean. The program is designed to support the improvement in the quality education of the partner countries and to strengthen their ties with Korea.

 

Program Objectives

  • To develop educational activities relevant to the needs and conditions of partner countries in the field of basic education, including math, science, ICT and Korean
  • To empower volunteer teachers through their involvement in the global education
  • To foster mutual exchanges in education

 

Volunteer Qualification

  • Certified teachers in-service at primary or secondary schools
  • Graduates with teacher certification for primary or secondary schools
  • Program Length : 1-3 Years

*   All volunteers must be certified in the subjects relevant to the curriculum of

the designated countries.
